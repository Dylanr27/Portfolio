Project - Game - Winter - 22

A few years ago I played my first rougelike called Dead Cells published by Motion twin. Dead Cells is a side scrolling, hack and slash, rougelike with tons of variety in the weapons, abilities, and routes you get to use through each playrough. Each time you failed, you would have to restart but your character would in most cases have gained abilities or weapons that they could utilize from the start or access quickly on there next playthrough. I found this gameplay loop along with the game being farely challenging to be a model for a very fun game. Ive since played other rougelikes such as Hades, Risk of Rain, and most recently Inscryption, and its safe to say ive become quite the fan of this genre. I believe a rougelike type of game would be a good place for me to start. My current thoughts are that I would look at Dead Cells as inspiration and go with a side scrolling type of game. I used to love castle crashers when i was younger, and i think a rougelike with that art style would be really cool. The medival theme is kind of already done by both dead cells and castle crashers, so perhaps a direction i could take my game could be having futuristic cyberpunk style weapons and abilites. A side scrolling cyberpunk Dead Cells with castle crashers art style sounds like something id love to make.
